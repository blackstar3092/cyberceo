Cyber learning that adapts to every audience.

CyberCEO builds curriculum for elementary students, senior citizens, and staff audiences, blending foundational safety topics with interactive activities.

Curriculum Pillars

Core themes: password safety, phishing awareness, data privacy, device protection, digital citizenship, and incident response basics.

Elementary Assembly

Interactive, high-energy sessions

Intro to Cybersecurity

Fast-paced assembly that introduces cyber concepts through stories, visuals, and live demos.

Kahoot! Knowledge Checks

Competitive Kahoots games reinforce safe password habits and phishing awareness.

Safe Online Behavior

Age-appropriate digital citizenship and how to ask for help when something feels wrong online.

Senior Curriculum

Patient, practical, and supportive

Scam Defense Basics

Recognizing phishing emails, text scams, and phone fraud with real examples.

Bingo for Cyber Safety

A bingo-style learning activity designed for seniors to reinforce key takeaways.

Everyday Device Help

Step-by-step guidance on updates, account recovery, and privacy settings.

Staff Training

Supporting educators and administrators

Staff workshops focus on phishing prevention, secure document handling, and response playbooks for common incidents. Materials are aligned with district needs.

High School Panel

Career and applied learning

Panels connect students with cybersecurity professionals across industry, higher education, and public service to explore pathways and real-world challenges.
