Why It Matters

1 in 3 people are targeted by cyber scams each year.

CyberCEO equips communities with the awareness and practical steps needed to reduce risk and respond quickly when threats appear.

Vision

Cyber confidence for every community.

We deliver real-world cybersecurity education that is practical, age-appropriate, and designed for long-term resilience in schools and public spaces.

Awareness Build habits that prevent phishing and scams.
Confidence Teach students and families how to act quickly.

Quick Summary

Curriculum at a glance

Elementary assemblies use stories, visuals, and Kahoots games to teach cyber basics. Senior sessions focus on scam prevention, device safety, and bingo-based learning.

Staff training covers phishing defense and incident response, while high school panels connect students with cybersecurity professionals.
