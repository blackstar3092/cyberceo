Collaboration across sectors to prepare high school students.

Our panel and partnership model connects students with cybersecurity leaders from education, industry, government, and nonprofit organizations.

Panel Focus

We highlight real-world career paths, practical problem solving, and the role of cybersecurity in public service and private industry.

Partner Sectors

Who we collaborate with

Education

School districts, teachers, and CTE pathway leaders.

Industry

Cybersecurity professionals, engineers, and analysts from local companies.

Public Sector

Government and public safety partners focused on digital safety.

Nonprofits

Community organizations that help us reach families and senior residents.

Panel Format

Designed for student impact

Panels include moderated Q&A, student-submitted questions, and a focus on real incidents and ethical decision-making in cybersecurity.

Outcomes

Students leave with clearer career pathways, an understanding of certifications, and tangible steps they can take in high school to prepare for cyber careers.
