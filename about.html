History, mission, and the measurable arc of CyberCEO.

CyberCEO is an outreach nonprofit established in Summer 2023 to educate the broader community about cyber and information security. We are aligned with the Information and Communication Technology CTE pathway and focus on building digital resilience.

Mission

Deliver practical, engaging cybersecurity education to students, staff, and senior communities while building a pathway for high school leaders to serve.

Vision

A future where every local school and community center has the knowledge to stay safe online and the confidence to respond to cyber threats.

Timeline

Year-by-year milestones

2023 · Launch Year

Established CyberCEO, assembled the student leadership team, and built the first Intro to Cybersecurity workshop for elementary and senior audiences.

2024 · Expansion Year

Scaled workshops to multiple elementary campuses and launched public library sessions. Expanded training for staff and seniors.

2025 · Community Impact Year

Delivered 1350+ student sessions, 45+ staff trainings, and reached 800+ senior citizens through targeted outreach.

2026 · Growth & Partnerships

Expanded programming to 1600+ students, maintained staff and senior training impact, and planned new library and high school initiatives.

Goals Ahead

Next-stage growth for CyberCEO

Scale curriculum across additional campuses and publish a public toolkit for staff training that local schools can adopt independently.

Formalize a mentorship track for student instructors and strengthen partnerships with school districts, public libraries, and cybersecurity industry leaders.
